Mathematical model of a premixed flame


Assumptions:

  • One dimensional steady subsonic flow.
  • Properties of component gases depend upon temperature.
  • Ideal gas behavior is valid for all the gases.
  • Flame is freely propagating.
  • No heat losses by radiation occur from the flame.


Model:

  • Governing equations of continuity, momentum, energy, and species are written.
  • Chemical kinetics is modeled using a readymade mechanism called GRI2.11 [1].


Boundary conditions:

    • Inflow boundary:
      • Known inlet velocity
      • Known temperature
      • Known mass fractions of CH4, O2, and N2

  • Outflow boundary:
    • Communicates with the ambient
    • Pressure is 101325 Pa
