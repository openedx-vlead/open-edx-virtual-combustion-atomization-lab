Conclusions



    • Cold flow visualization reveals an increase in the jet width with swirl addition.
      • With increase in swirl number, the ambient air entrainment increases and as a result, the jet width also increases.

    • The visible flame length
      • decreases with MFR for buoyancy dominant regime (MFR = 3 � 30)
      • remains almost constant for momentum dominant regime (MFR = 30 � 80)

    • The visible flame length decreases when swirl is increased.

    • Increased swirl results in well mixed zones near burner exit and flame stabilization near burner exit.

  • Blowout limit extends with swirl number.
