Precautions



    • Ensure that there is no leakage in the fuel line.

    • Turn on the exhaust fan before starting the experiment.

    • Check whether the valves are functioning properly.

    • Camera has to be kept at sufficient distance from the flame.

    • After completing the experiment, turn off the valve in the fuel cylinder and continue the air flow for certain time.

    • Hood should be used for evacuation of the exhaust gas.

  • Keep the fire extinguisher nearby the experimental setup.
